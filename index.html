The Timelessness of Tetris

Tetris is one of my favorite games to play, both with others and on my own. You'd think that a simple game about clearing lines of blocks released in 1984 and reskinned so many times throughout history would eventually go out of fashion, but something about the way that the game's gradual difficulty curve pushes the player to improve their own skills, as well as the community you can build with others while playing the game brings me back to it on multiple occasions.

Jacob Geller's video, "Tetris Effect and Other Games with Immaculate Vibes," expresses a similarly profound respect and fondness for the game.
